Ninja Sweeper

Welcome to Ninja Sweeper!

How to Play:

  • Click (press) the lil bushsters to clear the board, avoiding ninja
  • Right-click (long press) on bushels to place shuriken or "flags" on suspected ninja locations
    • Squares with shuriken placed on them are unclickable
    • Right-click (long press) again to remove the shuriken
  • Numbers on a square indicate how many ninjas surround it
  • Use the numbers to deduce and uncover all the unoccupied squares to win!

For a more in depth tutorial, find the i.
